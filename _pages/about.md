I am a 4th year PhD student in Applied Physics at [Caltech](https://www.caltech.edu/), advised by [Prof. Garnet Kin-Lic Chan](https://www.chan-lab.caltech.edu/). My research focuses on developing and applying **tensor network** and **machine learning** methods for **quantum many-body systems**.

## Research Interests

- **Tensor Network Methods**: Developing novel tensor network states and algorithms for strongly correlated quantum systems.
- **Machine Learning for Quantum Physics**: Combining neural network architectures with tensor network structures to create expressive variational ansatze.
- **Variational Monte Carlo**: Building high-performance computational tools for variational optimization of quantum states.

## Software

- [**vmc_torch**](https://github.com/sjdu10/vmc_torch): A high-performance, parallelized Variational Monte Carlo library built on PyTorch, supporting Neural Quantum States (NQS), Tensor Network States (TNS), and hybrid models. See the [Portfolio](/portfolio/) page for more details.
